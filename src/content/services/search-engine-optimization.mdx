## Page Two Is Where Businesses Go to Die

Someone just searched for exactly what you offer. They typed the words, hit enter, and started scrolling through results. Your competitors appeared. You didn't.

That customer is now someone else's customer.

## The Reality of Modern SEO

SEO has evolved far beyond keyword stuffing and link schemes. Google's algorithms now evaluate hundreds of factors, prioritizing sites that genuinely serve user intent. Gaming the system no longer works. Building genuine authority does.

**Search intent drives rankings.** Google doesn't just match keywords—it understands what users actually want. Content that answers real questions outranks content that merely contains keywords.

**Technical foundation matters.** Site speed, mobile experience, crawlability, security—these technical factors determine whether Google can even evaluate your content. Poor technical SEO sabotages everything else.

**Authority is earned over time.** Quality backlinks, consistent content, strong engagement signals—search authority compounds. The sites winning today started building years ago.

**Local SEO is its own game.** For service businesses, appearing in the map pack can matter more than organic rankings. Local SEO requires specific tactics: Google Business Profile optimization, review management, local citations.
