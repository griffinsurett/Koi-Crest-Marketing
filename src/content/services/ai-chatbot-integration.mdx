## The Question at 3 AM

Your customer has a question. It's 3 AM. They're about to make a purchase—or walk away forever. Nobody's answering the phone. Email will take hours. Live chat shows "offline."

They leave. You'll never know they were there.

This happens constantly. Customers have questions at inconvenient times. Visitors need help when you're busy with other customers. Leads require immediate response while you're asleep, at dinner, on vacation.

AI chatbots change this equation. Intelligent, always-available assistance that handles common questions, qualifies leads, and provides support—even when you can't.

## Why Most Chatbots Fail

The chatbot graveyard is full of frustrated customers and wasted investments. Understanding why helps avoid the same mistakes.

**Scripted rigidity.** Decision-tree chatbots that break when users ask anything unexpected. "I didn't understand that" loops that frustrate rather than help.

**Misaligned expectations.** Chatbots pretending to be human, failing the test, and destroying trust. Transparency about AI builds more confidence than deception.

**Wrong use cases.** Chatbots handling complex issues they can't resolve, making problems worse before humans intervene. AI should augment human support, not replace it entirely.

**No escalation path.** Conversations that dead-end without resolution. No handoff to human support. Customers trapped in AI purgatory.
