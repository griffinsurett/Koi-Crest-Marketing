You know the pattern. New Year's rush fills your gym with motivated members. By March, half have ghosted. By summer, you're back to the same core group wondering how to grow.

## Why Gym Marketing Is a Lead Generation Game

The fitness industry runs on membership. Every empty spot in class, every unused locker, every piece of equipment sitting idle — that's revenue you're leaving on the table. Effective marketing turns strangers into members:

- **High churn reality** — even great gyms lose 30-50% of members annually; you need constant new leads
- **Lifestyle purchases** — people join gyms based on aspiration and emotion, not logic
- **Local competition** — in most areas, members have 10+ options within a few miles
- **Trial conversions** — getting someone in the door is just step one; converting trials to memberships is the real game
- **Community building** — the gyms that create community retain members; marketing should reflect that
