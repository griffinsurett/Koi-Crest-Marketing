## Why Nonprofit Marketing Is Different

Marketing for nonprofits isn't about selling products — it's about inspiring action. You need to tell stories that move people to donate, volunteer, and spread the word. But you also need to do it on a budget:

- **Limited resources** — every dollar spent on marketing is a dollar not spent on mission
- **Multiple audiences** — donors, volunteers, beneficiaries, and community partners all need different messages
- **Trust is paramount** — people need to know their donations and time are making real impact
- **Grant requirements** — many funders want to see professional branding and clear communication
- **Volunteer engagement** — attracting and retaining volunteers requires consistent outreach
