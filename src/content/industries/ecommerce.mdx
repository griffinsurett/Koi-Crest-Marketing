## Why eCommerce Marketing Is a Multi-Channel War

Selling online isn't as simple as "build it and they will come." You're competing with millions of stores for attention, and attention is expensive. Success requires orchestrating multiple channels into a cohesive system:

- **Ad costs keep rising** — Facebook, Instagram, and Google ads get more expensive every year
- **Email is your owned asset** — social platforms can change algorithms; your email list is yours forever
- **Content builds trust** — customers research before buying; your content should answer their questions
- **Retention beats acquisition** — it costs 5x more to acquire a new customer than to keep an existing one
- **Brand matters** — in a sea of options, strong branding is what makes customers choose (and pay more for) you
