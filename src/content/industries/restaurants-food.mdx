## Why Restaurant Marketing Is a Daily Battle

The food industry moves fast. You're not just competing with other restaurants — you're competing with grocery stores, meal kits, and the convenience of staying home. Every single day, you need to give people a reason to choose you:

- **Razor-thin margins** — every empty seat directly impacts your bottom line
- **Daily decision-making** — customers decide where to eat in the moment, often on their phones
- **Visual appetite** — people eat with their eyes first; your food needs to look irresistible online
- **Review culture** — one bad Yelp review can cost you thousands in lost business
- **Local competition** — in a 5-mile radius, you might have 50+ alternatives fighting for the same customers
